Quick Look: Flower Mart

A local garden center enlisted my help to update the look of their online greenhouse inventory. With a vision of clean but contemporary product photography in mind, I spent a number of mornings photographing their new stock items. The result? Inventory photography can be artistic, too.

Alocasia

Creating a DIY photobox in the Flower Mart greenhouse suited the vibe of this boutique plant locale. The aim for the client was an updated, contemporary look.
